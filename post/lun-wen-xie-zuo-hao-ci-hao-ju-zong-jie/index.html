论文写作好词好句总结

2021-07-25 4 min read

常见同义词替换

词组区

has been proposed to = be thought to = be considered 被认为
can/could = be capable of = be sufficient to 能够
account for = cause = pose
health and well-being 一生安康
help = aid in = serve as a referrence to 对...有帮助
lack of = a paucity of = few = little 缺乏
plays a important role on = be essential for 对...重要的
notion = insight = study 概念，观点
in line with = be aligned with = high consistency of与...一致

单词区

动词区(V.)

regulate = modulate = program V.调节
display = function = has emerged to be = perform = produce (response/...function) = employ = query = implement V. 表达..功能
underpin = consolidate = reinforce V. 加强 加固(某种观点)
resemble V. 类似，像
different = vary = be inconsistent with V. 与...不同
suggest = illustrate = reveal = represent = uncover = illuminate = indicate = found = support = clarify = observe = emphasize V. 表明(结果)
reflect = mirror 反映、反射
promote = facilitate = stablize = harbor V. 促进
prevent = obliterate = restrain = suppress = attenuate V. 阻碍
upregulate = improve = elevate = increase = rise = accelerateV. 提升，增加
downregulate = decrease = degrade = reduce = decline = drop = decelerate V. 降低，减少
treated = administrated = undergone V. (经历)给药
affect = modify = have influence on = impact V. 影响...
advance V. 提前
delay V. 推迟
study = explore = invest = exploit V. 研究
generate V. 产生，生成
contain = comprise = be consisted of = consisting of = constitute V. 包含，由...组成

名词区(n.)

fashion = pattern = manner 方式、模式
eg. in a dose-dependent manner 呈剂量依赖性模式
the involvement of = relationship

连词区

however = whereas 然而
due to = because = bacause of = since 因为
Thus = therefore = accordingly

形容词、副词区

significantly = remarkably = notably = striking 显著地
with respect to = about = in relation to = relative = related = in response to 相关的
temporal = transient 暂时的
in fact = actually 事实上
herein 本文中，于此处
thoroughly = entirely 彻底地
increased = increasingly = incremental 逐渐的，递增的
decreased = reduced 逐渐减少的
interestingly = intriguingly 有趣地

前言/摘要 Introduction/Abstract

A growing body of evidence revealed that ... 越来越多的证据表明
It is well known that ... 众所周知的
Thus far 迄今为止
be manifested at ... 体现在....
As the expansion of ... 随着某方面研究的增加...
It is now well understood that .... ...仍有缺陷
Consistent with the findings that ..., the studies in ... showed ....
be attributed to ... 对...有作用
we wished to determine whether ..., whether ..., whether ... (...中是正常陈述句)

阐述方法 Materious and method

A(细胞) placed into B(培养环境) 置于
mouse were sacrificed at .... 在...时间杀一批老鼠
...(组织) were removed, frozen ....
... were harvested for RNA and whole-cell protein 收细胞提取RNA和蛋白质

阐述结果 Result

常用句型

We also point to ways in which ... 我们也指出了
There are known significant differences in ... 已知在...领域存在显著差异
likely due 可能是因为(可作插入语)
in part 一部分(可作插入语)
eg. Importantly, there are known sex differences in the expression of circadian locomotor activity and molecular organization of the suprachiasmatic nucleus, likely due, in part, to the actions of circulating estrogens.
... without marked change ...无显著性差异

常用词组

response to misalignment 反应失调
energy consumption and expenditure 能量的消耗和支出
like to = might 可能
