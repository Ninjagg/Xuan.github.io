2021-07-25 1 min read

常见同义词替换

词组区

be thought to = be considered 被认为

单词区

阐述结果 Result

常用句型

We also point to ways in which ... 我们也指出了
There are known significant differences in ... 已知在...领域存在显著差异
likely due 可能是因为(可作插入语)
in part 一部分(可作插入语)
eg. Importantly, there are known sex differences in the expression of circadian locomotor activity and molecular organization of the suprachiasmatic nucleus, likely due, in part, to the actions of circulating estrogens.
